Open Play

  • 9:00pm - 10:30pm Fridays
  • 7:00pm - 10:30pm Sundays
Whether you are interested in casual play or competitive matches, open play is a great for players of all skill levels.

To participate, the drop-in fee is $5 but the fee is waived for due-paying members. The drop-in fee is also waived for first your first three sessions to allow new players a chance to "test drive".

Currently, sessions take place at the RSF.

Novice Training

  • 7:00pm - 8:00pm Fridays
Created for new players to practice and learn basic table tennis skills and techniques. Our dedicated coaching team will guide you through a multitude of drills as well as some basic strategy.

To participate, the drop-in fee is $5 but the fee is waived for due-paying members. The drop-in fee is also waived for first your first three sessions to allow new players a chance to "test drive".

Currently, sessions take place at the RSF.

Advanced Training

  • 8:00pm - 9:00pm Fridays
Designed for experienced players to take their game to the next level and become a better competitive player. Players from the competitive team will be there to help train and practice with as well as our coaching team.

To participate, the drop-in fee is $8 but the fee is waived for due-paying members. The drop-in fee is also waived for first your first three sessions to allow new players a chance to "test drive".

Currently, sessions take place at the RSF.

Private Training

  • Not Currently Offered
Whether you are a beginner or advanced player, we have many players available for one-on-one training.

Depending on the coach, the price varies.

Tournaments
